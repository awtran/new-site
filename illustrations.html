My attempts at getting better with the pen tool.

Overview

Digital art has always fascinated me and it was something I wanted to get better at. So I challenged myself: use only the shape and pen tools. And even more so, only work with Figma.

I was (and still am) really inspired by some of the great illustrators— names like Meg Roubichard and Meredith Schomburg. Their influences are noticeable in some of my illustrations.

Hope you enjoy! 👍
