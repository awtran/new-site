Overwatch Team Builds

Quickly find the best team compositions for any map, any game mode.

"Heroes never die."

Overwatch — in its main form — is a matchup between two teams, each consisting of 6 heroes. With 20+ maps and 20+ heroes, it can take hours of experience or research to finally figure out the subtle advantages a given hero can have on a given map.

The goal was to create an app for displaying optimal team builds to players based on game mode and map.
