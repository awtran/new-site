Another Podcast?

Yes, I am joining the podcast craze and feeling no shame. I love podcasts, design ones especially. If you're a regular listener of shows like Design Details or really anything from the Spec Network then you'd understand.

But as much as I found inspiration from the amazing designers that guest starred in the episodes, there was a lack of reassurance that I wasn't alone in my process. So I set out to start my own podcast to share my insights—as well as others—to encourage and motivate designers who are new to the industry.

Have a listen! 🎧
